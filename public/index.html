Accommodation

One of PGL larger centres, Caythorpe has up to 23 different thrilling activities to experience, making this a perfect multi activity choice for your family. Thanks to our purpose-built lakes we have an ideal mix of watersports and land-based activities. There is also an amazing high ropes activity area at the heart of the centre, archery ranges, a new tunnel trail activity, an outdoor sports pitch and some great weather-proof spaces including a sports hall and an indoor firing range.

The Staff

With 60 years of experience, PGL’s commitment to staff training and development is unsurpassed. Our staff must be adept at bringing out the very best in children, while keeping them safe and happy. So PGL ensure each one is trained and equipped with the skills, knowledge and attitude to give your children the time of their lives.

Trained Instructors take responsibility during activity sessions. We have an excellent safety record, and PGL insist our Instructors enforce the highest possible safety standards. Where a formal qualification exists, Instructors must hold it and Trainee Instructors must study for it. Where no recognised qualifications exist, they carry out verified in-house training and assessments.
